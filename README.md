## **What Is Thinkific? Is It Worth It?**

It is publicly listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange ([TSX: THNC](https://money.tmx.com/en/quote/THNC)) and serves over **50,000 course creators** across 164 countries. The platform has facilitated more than **$1 billion in course revenue** generated by its creators, a figure that underscores both the scale of the platform and the genuine commercial outcomes its tools enable.

Thinkific's core product is a toolkit for creating, selling, and delivering online courses, communities, memberships, digital downloads, coaching programs, and webinars, all under a single branded school managed through one dashboard. The platform is designed to give creators full ownership of their content, student relationships, and revenue, with 0% platform transaction fees (using TCommerce) and no algorithmic discoverability required to succeed.

**What makes Thinkific stand out in 2026:**

- **0% platform transaction fees.** Thinkific does not take a cut of your course revenue when you use TCommerce, every dollar a student pays (minus standard Stripe processing fees) goes directly to you. This is a foundational advantage over platforms like Udemy (which keeps 50–75% of revenue from marketplace sales) and even Teachable's free plan (which charges 10% + $1 per transaction).
- **Unlimited courses and students.** All Thinkific paid plans allow unlimited courses and, up to the 10,000 student cap on Basic/Start/Grow, unlimited student enrollments. The Plus plan removes the student cap entirely for enterprise-scale operations.
- **AI-powered course creation.** Thinkific's AI tools (available on all paid plans) help creators draft course outlines, generate lesson content, and build structured curriculum from scratch, significantly reducing the time from idea to published course. This is a meaningful differentiator in 2026 for creators who are content experts but not professional instructional designers.
- **Built-in community features.** Every Thinkific plan includes community spaces where enrolled students can connect, discuss, and engage, reducing the need for separate Discord servers or Facebook groups. Community spaces are included within the course platform, creating a unified learner experience.
- **Learning commerce depth.** Beyond basic course delivery, Thinkific supports memberships, bundles, post-purchase upsells, payment plans, order bumps, affiliate programs, B2B group orders, and bulk enrollments, giving course creators the same revenue diversification tools that most competing platforms require expensive upgrades or external tools to access.

You build an owned audience, not a rented one. Students are yours to communicate with, retain, and upsell, indefinitely.
- **Thinkific Payments / TCommerce.** Thinkific's native payment processor eliminates the external Stripe surcharge and simplifies financial management. It supports one-time purchases, subscriptions, payment plans, free trials, and order bumps natively.
- **Deeply structured course builder.** Thinkific's course builder supports multiple content types, video, audio, PDF, text, live lessons, quizzes, assignments, downloadable files, and surveys, organized into structured sections and chapters with drip scheduling. This depth of structure makes Thinkific particularly well-suited to creators building professional-grade, accreditation-adjacent learning experiences.
